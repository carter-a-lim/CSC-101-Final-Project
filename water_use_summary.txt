============================================================
 CALIFORNIA WATER USE ANALYSIS - METHOD EXPLANATION
============================================================
This report analyzes residential water usage across suppliers.
All calculations are based on the following formulas:

• Efficiency (%) = ((Total Residential Use - Potable Use) / Total Residential Use) * 100
   -> Represents the share of recycled/nonpotable water.

• Population Served = (Total Residential Use / (R-GPCD * 365))
   -> Estimates number of residents served using daily per-capita consumption.

• Overuse Flag = Supplier with R-GPCD above regional median AND efficiency < 5%
   -> Identifies potential overconsumption areas.

• Regional Classification: based on supplier name keywords (Southern, Central, Northern California)
